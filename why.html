Why I Like Cats

Cats are the best for a number of reasons. Reason 1, they do more funny actions. Cats are always slapping stuff off tables, slapping other animals, falling off everything, trying to climb everywhere, and more. They also look better when they play with toys. For example a cat playing with a laser pointer looks better than you playing fetch with your dog. Cats are just funnier. They are just better. They also just look really cute (except the ugly cats, they do not look cute).
